All research projects funded by the Gulf of Mexico Research Initiative (GoMRI) are required to make data publically accessible. Datasets can be submitted directly to GRIIDC, however, your dataset may also be submitted to a national data archival center. If your dataset is submitted to a national data archive center you must still register your dataset with the GRIIDC system so that the dataset can be catalogued with other datasets produced by GoMRI research projects. If you will be submitting your dataset to a national data center not on the list provided, please specify the data center and associated URL in the “other” field.
